Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Jewelry FAQ section. Here, you'll find answers to the most common questions about our products, services, and policies. If you need further assistance, please feel free to contact us.

Our jewelry is crafted from high-quality materials including sterling silver, 14k and 18k gold, platinum, and genuine gemstones. We ensure all materials are ethically sourced and meet industry standards. Each piece is carefully inspected to guarantee durability and beauty.
To keep your jewelry looking its best, store it in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight. Clean regularly with a soft cloth and avoid contact with harsh chemicals, perfumes, and lotions. For pieces with gemstones, use a gentle soap and water solution.
